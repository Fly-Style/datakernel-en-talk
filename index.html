I/O : CSP & Datastream

    CSP

  • Inspired by Golang channels
  • Compined "push-pull" mechanic
  • Performance with middle-size byte buffers
  • Effective async back-pressure
  • Simple developer experience
  • Fast enough, but slower than Datastream

    Datastream

  • Reactive and asynchronous
  • It works по with "Tell Don't Ask" principle
  • Optimized to work with small pieces of data
  • Effective oveload control
  • Damn good and fast but DX is harder
  • Event Loop is integrated as main engine

CSP и Datastream are comparable with each other
